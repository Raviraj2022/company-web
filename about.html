About Us

Property First has been awarded as Real Estate Consultant of the year by Economic Times Realty in 2024 and 2025. Property First is a top channel partner with Grade A developers in Bangalore (Corporate Office), Dubai, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Pune, Goa, Hyderabad, Chennai.

Who We Are

Award-winning real estate consultants, recognized by The Economic Times Realty (2024 & 2025). Headquartered in Bangalore, with presence across Mumbai, Pune, Goa, Dubai, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Delhi NCR. We partner with Grade-A developers to bring clients the finest properties in every location.

What We Do

We secure the best deals and verified properties for our clients every time. With strong industry connections, we offer exclusive inventory and high-value investments that are legally clear and have strong appreciation potential. Our goal is to make property investment simple, safe, and rewarding.

Hi, I’m Bhavesh Kothari

Founder & CEO

Founded in 2017 by Mr Bhavesh Kothari, a seasoned real estate professional with over 13 years of experience in real estate, Property First is a real estate consulting firm that believes in establishing life term relations with its clients by helping them find the right property with right insights and at right price which can be noted with the fact that 70% of its clients are repeat customers.

Property First uses its experience and connect in the industry to get the best deals and inventory for the clients in their chosen locality/ Project. Properties are carefully selected based on their appreciation potential, builder reputation and liveability index of the community. This careful selection of properties is the sole reason that most clients make a average return of 25- 30% per year on these investments and on some Properties, even upto 100% in a year. This is the sole reason why clients and investors prefer Property First and 70% of its clients are repeat customers (Do check out our customer testimonial videos) .

Property First has very high standards while hiring and RMs go through a rigorous training to become an expert. Currently, Property First has its presence in Bangalore (Corporate Office), Dubai (selling Dubai properties), Mumbai, Pune, Goa, Dubai, Hyderabad, Chennai, Delhi NCR and has aggressive expansion plans to other geographies as well.

Property First is a CRISIL rated firm and one of the top channel partner firms with most reputed developers (do check out awards section by clicking here) and has won Jury recommendation for Best real estate consulting Firm (National) at the Economic Times Real Estate Conclave 2024 and 2025. Property First has also been awarded South India’s best luxury real estate consulting firm by Int’l Brand Equity in 2019-20, and again in 2020-2021. Property First has been nominated by RICS (Royal Institute for Chartered Surveyors) as India’s best real estate consulting firm in years 2023 & again in 2024.

We still believe we have a long way to go achieve our vision of being the most trusted real estate brand in the geographies we are present.
